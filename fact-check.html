Our Fact Checking Guidelines

Transparent standards for verifying information and maintaining accuracy

Methodology

  • Multiple independent sources are consulted for each claim
  • Primary sources are prioritized over secondary reports
  • Expert consultation is sought for technical topics
  • Clear explanations of methodology are provided

Rating System

TRUE Claim is accurate and supported by evidence
FALSE Claim is incorrect and contradicted by evidence
MIXED Claim contains both true and false elements
MISLEADING Claim is technically true but presented deceptively
